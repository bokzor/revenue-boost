## Feature-by-Feature Breakdown

### Campaign Types & Templates

**Revenue Boost** shines with its comprehensive library of 15 specialized templates, each optimized for specific conversion goals:

- **Lead Generation**: Newsletter popups, exit-intent captures
- **Gamification**: Interactive spin-to-win wheels and scratch cards
- **Sales & Urgency**: Flash sales, countdown timers, limited-time offers
- **Conversion Boosters**: Free shipping bars, [cart abandonment](https://revenue-boost.app/blog/cart-abandonment-popups-on-shopify-email-capture-flows-that-recover-revenue-2025-2026-examples) recovery
- **Upsells**: Multiple product upsell layouts with 10 design options
- **Trust Building**: Social proof notifications, announcement bars

**Wisepops** offers a solid template selection focused on traditional popups and forms, with good customization options. However, it lacks the specialized gamification elements and free shipping bars that make Revenue Boost stand out.

### Targeting & Triggers

Both platforms excel in targeting capabilities, but with different approaches:

**Revenue Boost** offers:
- 8 different trigger types (page load, exit intent, scroll depth, idle timer, add to cart, cart drawer open, cart value, product view)
- Advanced audience segmentation (device, OS, browser, connection type, Shopify customer segments)
- Geographic targeting with include/exclude modes
- Sophisticated frequency capping to prevent popup fatigue

**Wisepops** provides:
- 30+ targeting rules including Shopify tags, cart value, page URL, location, traffic source
- Good behavioral targeting options
- Solid segmentation capabilities

Both apps perform well here, but Revenue Boost's native integration with Shopify customer segments gives it an edge for merchants using Shopify's built-in customer management.

### Discount Management

This is where **Revenue Boost** truly pulls ahead:

**Revenue Boost Features:**
- Automatic discount creation with multiple strategies (simple, bundle, tiered, BOGO, free gift)
- Native Shopify discount codes that sync automatically
- Smart discount behaviors (auto-apply, email-specific codes)
- Advanced discount constraints and expiry management
- Custom branded prefixes for discount codes

**Wisepops:**
- Manual discount code setup
- Basic discount display functionality
- Requires more manual intervention

Revenue Boost's automated discount system saves merchants significant time and ensures consistent promotion management across all campaigns.

### Gamification

**Revenue Boost** leads the pack with engaging gamification features:
- **Spin-to-Win**: Fully customizable wheel with 2+ segments, win probability control, and prize configuration
- **Scratch Cards**: Interactive scratch-to-reveal experiences
- Configurable spin duration, visual effects, and result animations
- Email capture integration with prize redemption

**Wisepops** offers basic gamification but lacks the depth and customization options found in Revenue Boost.

### Analytics & Reporting

Both platforms provide robust analytics:

**Revenue Boost:**
- Campaign performance metrics
- Conversion tracking
- Revenue attribution
- A/B testing with statistical significance

**Wisepops:**
- Advanced analytics platform
- Revenue tracking
- Customer journey analysis
- Experiment platform with controlled tests

Wisepops has slightly more sophisticated analytics for enterprise users, while Revenue Boost provides everything most merchants need to track performance effectively.
