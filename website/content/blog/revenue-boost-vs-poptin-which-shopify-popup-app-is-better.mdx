## Feature-by-Feature Breakdown

### 1. Campaign Variety & Templates

**Revenue Boost** excels with 15 professionally designed campaign templates, each optimized for specific conversion goals:

- **Lead Generation**: Newsletter popups, exit-intent forms
- **Gamification**: Spin-to-win wheels, interactive scratch cards
- **Sales & Urgency**: Flash sales, countdown timers
- **Conversion Boosters**: Free shipping bars, [cart abandonment](https://revenue-boost.app/blog/cart-abandonment-popups-on-shopify-email-capture-flows-that-recover-revenue-2025-2026-examples) recovery
- **Upsells**: Multiple layout options (classic, minimal slide-up, premium fullscreen)
- **Trust Building**: Real-time social proof notifications

**Poptin** offers standard popup types including exit-intent popups, email capture forms, and spin-to-wheel campaigns, but lacks the variety and specialized templates that Revenue Boost provides.

### 2. Smart Targeting Capabilities

**Revenue Boost** features an advanced targeting engine with:

- **9+ Trigger Types**: Page load, exit-intent, scroll depth, idle timer, add to cart, cart value, product view, and custom events
- **AND/OR Logic**: Combine triggers for complex scenarios
- **Advanced Audience Rules**: Device types, operating systems, browsers, connection types
- **Shopify Segments Integration**: Target VIPs, repeat buyers, and custom segments
- **Geographic Targeting**: Include/exclude specific countries
- **Frequency Capping**: Control display limits per session and day

**Poptin** provides basic targeting with exit-intent, time-based, and scroll-based triggers, along with standard display rules for geolocation and traffic sources.

### 3. Gamification Features

**Revenue Boost** stands out with comprehensive gamification options:

- **Spin-to-Win Wheel**: Fully customizable with 2+ segments, probability control, configurable prizes, and styling options
- **Scratch Cards**: Interactive scratch-to-reveal experiences
- **Advanced Configuration**: Spin duration, minimum rotations, glow effects, result icons
- **Email Integration**: Seamlessly collect emails and assign discount codes

**Poptin** offers only basic spin-to-wheel functionality with limited customization options.

### 4. Discount Management

**Revenue Boost** provides a comprehensive discount engine with native Shopify integration:

- **5 Discount Strategies**: Simple, bundle, tiered, BOGO, free gift
- **Multiple Value Types**: Percentage, fixed amount, free shipping
- **Advanced Behaviors**: Auto-apply, email assignment, usage limits
- **Discount Stacking Control**: Combine with other discounts as needed
- **Customer Segmentation**: Target specific customer groups

**Poptin** has basic discount functionality but lacks the advanced features and deep Shopify integration that Revenue Boost offers.

### 5. Analytics & Optimization

**Revenue Boost** includes:

- Detailed conversion tracking
- Revenue attribution
- A/B testing with statistical significance
- Campaign performance metrics
- Customer behavior insights

**Poptin** offers basic analytics and conversion tracking but lacks the depth and revenue-focused metrics that Revenue Boost provides.
